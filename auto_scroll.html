Privacy Policy

Auto Scroll for Safari - QuickRead
Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Auto Scroll for Safari - QuickRead is built with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. This app works entirely on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Auto Scroll for Safari - QuickRead does not collect, access, or store any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Browsing history or website URLs you visit
  • Search queries or web content
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

2. How the App Works

Auto Scroll for Safari operates as a Safari extension that runs entirely on your local device. The app:

  • Functions completely offline after installation
  • Does not communicate with any external servers
  • Does not transmit any data over the internet
  • Stores all settings and preferences locally on your device only

3. Data Storage

The only data stored by Auto Scroll for Safari is your personal preferences and settings, which include:

  • Scroll mode preference (Smooth Scroll or Page Scroll)
  • Scroll speed settings
  • Page scroll distance and pause duration
  • Extension on/off state

Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device using Safari's standard extension storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

4. No Third-Party Services

Auto Scroll for Safari does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage or backup services

5. No Advertisements

This app does not display any advertisements. There are no ad networks, no tracking pixels, and no ad-related data collection of any kind.

6. Permissions

Auto Scroll for Safari requires the following Safari extension permissions to function:

  • Access to webpage content: Required to control scrolling on the pages you visit. This access is used only to execute scrolling commands and is never used to read, collect, or transmit your browsing data.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and nothing else.

7. Children's Privacy

Auto Scroll for Safari does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Security

Since Auto Scroll for Safari does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers, there is no risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, or data leaks from our end. Your settings are protected by your device's built-in security features.

9. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You maintain complete control over the app's local settings, which you can reset or remove at any time by:

  • Disabling the extension in Safari settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

11. International Users

Auto Scroll for Safari is designed to be privacy-compliant worldwide, including but not limited to:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States
  • Other international privacy regulations

Since we do not collect any personal data, compliance with these regulations is inherently maintained.

12. Open Source and Transparency

We believe in transparency. The core functionality of Auto Scroll for Safari is straightforward: it provides scrolling controls on web pages you visit. There are no hidden features, no background processes collecting data, and no communication with external servers.
