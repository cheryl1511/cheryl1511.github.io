Privacy Policy
🛡️

We collect absolutely nothing.

TabUndo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. Your browsing is entirely yours.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No browsing history or visited URLs
  • No personal information (name, email, location)
  • No device identifiers or IP addresses
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No crash reports sent to any server

⚙️ How the Extensions Work

Both Safari extensions — Open Closed Tab and Reopen in Private — operate entirely on your device. All processing happens locally within Safari.

No data ever leaves your device. There are no servers, no databases, and no third-party SDKs involved.

🔗 Third-Party Services

TabUndo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any similar platforms.

There are no ads in this app, and no data is sold or shared with advertisers or any other parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

TabUndo is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk of any personal information being gathered from children or anyone else.

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the new version will be published on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Any future changes will maintain the same commitment to your privacy.
