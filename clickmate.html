Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RefreshMate ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our Safari extension application.

Information Collection and Use

RefreshMate is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

Data Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Refresh timer settings are stored locally on your device
  • Alert configurations are stored locally on your device
  • Text monitoring patterns are processed locally
  • Auto-click settings are stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers

Permissions

RefreshMate requires the following permissions to function:

  • Safari Extension Permission: To refresh pages and monitor content
  • Storage Permission: To save your preferences locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

Third-Party Services

RefreshMate does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Security

Since all data is processed and stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
