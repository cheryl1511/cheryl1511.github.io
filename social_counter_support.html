Privacy Policy
Your data is safe with us

Last updated: March 2026


Like Social Counter is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's exactly what we do and don't do:

  • We do not create user accounts within the app
  • We do not collect your name or email address
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • Facebook and TikTok connections are used solely to read public page statistics (likes/followers)
  • We do not share your data with any third parties
  • You can revoke access at any time via app Settings or your social media account's security settings
