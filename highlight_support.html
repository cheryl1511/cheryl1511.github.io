Getting started

Setting Up the Extension

1

Open Safari Settings

Go to Settings → Safari → Extensions on your iPhone or iPad.

2

Enable HighlightAnywhere

Toggle HighlightAnywhere on and grant permission to "All Websites" for the best experience.

3

Open any webpage in Safari

Tap the extension icon (puzzle piece) in the Safari toolbar, then tap HighlightAnywhere to activate.

4

Select text to begin

Long-press any text to select it. A color picker popup will appear — choose a color to highlight.

💡 Tip: Grant "Allow" permission for "All Websites" so the extension works on every page without asking each time.

HighlightAnywhere supports three quick selection modes:

Word — double-tap to select a single word.
Sentence — selects the full sentence containing the tapped word.
Paragraph — selects the entire paragraph with one tap.

You can also manually drag to select any custom range of text.

You can choose from three visual styles:

Full highlight — colored background behind the text.
Underline — colored line beneath the text, less intrusive.

Markdown (.md) — great for note-taking apps like Obsidian or Notion.
HTML (.html) — preserves styling, open in any browser.
