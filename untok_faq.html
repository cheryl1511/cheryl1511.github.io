Using UnTok

Try the following steps in order:

  • Force-close the app and reopen it
  • Log out of the app and log back in
  • Switch to a different Wi-Fi network, or turn Wi-Fi off and use mobile data
  • Disconnect from any VPN or proxy
  • Log out of TikTok on all web browsers, then try again
  • If you recently removed a large number of reposts, wait a few minutes before trying again

If none of the above work, contact us at code.and.play.vn@gmail.com.

Errors are almost always caused by a temporary issue on TikTok's end — not a problem with your account or UnTok. They typically clear up on their own. To speed things up:

  • Log out of the app and log back in
  • Switch to a different Wi-Fi network, or use mobile data instead
  • Log out of TikTok on all web browsers, then try again

If the error persists, TikTok may be experiencing an outage. Try again in 30–60 minutes.

Billing & Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings. Go to Settings → [Your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone and select UnTok to manage or cancel.

Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. Canceling stops the renewal — you will not be charged again.

Refunds are handled by Apple. If you have not used the subscription to remove any reposts, you may be eligible for a refund through Apple's standard refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Refunds will not be provided if you have used any portion of your subscription to remove reposts.
