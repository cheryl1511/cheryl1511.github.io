🔒 Zero Data Collection Guarantee

ClipStash is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data, clipboard content, or saved media. Everything remains strictly on your local device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ClipStash does not require account creation, registration, or personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (such as name, email address, phone number, or contacts).
  • Clipboard contents (clipboard text is processed strictly on-device to auto-detect media links).
  • Saved media, download history, or custom collections.
  • Location data, IP addresses, or device identifiers.
  • Usage telemetry or analytics tracking.

2. Local Data Processing & Storage

All core functions of ClipStash operate locally on your device:

  • Media Downloads & History: Any photos or videos saved using ClipStash are downloaded directly to your device storage. Download history and organized collections are stored locally using native on-device storage.
  • Clipboard Access: ClipStash accesses your device clipboard solely to detect copied links when you open or interact with the app. Clipboard content is never transmitted over the internet or sent to external servers.

3. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

ClipStash offers an optional premium upgrade (ClipStash Pro). In-app purchase transactions are processed securely by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) via RevenueCat framework.

We do not collect, access, or store your payment details, billing address, or credit card numbers. Payment handling is managed strictly by the store providers under their respective privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

ClipStash does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or data brokers. Network requests made by the app are limited strictly to fetching media files directly from public links requested by you, and verifying in-app purchase status with store services.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data collection occurs, ClipStash complies fully with global privacy regulations regarding minors.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
