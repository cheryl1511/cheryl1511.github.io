Zero Data Collection

DualCam Pro is built with a deep respect for your privacy. We believe your footage belongs to you and only you.

No Personal Information

DualCam Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, location data, usage statistics, or crash reports. We do not require account registration or login.

On-Device Processing

All video recording and processing happens entirely on your device. Your footage is never uploaded to any cloud service or shared with third parties by us.

The videos you record are saved directly to your Photos library using Apple's secure frameworks.

Required Permissions

DualCam Pro requests access to the following only for on-device functionality:

• Camera: To capture dual-lens or front/back video.

• Microphone: To record audio along with your video.

• Photos Library: To save your final video files directly to your device.

Third-Party Services

DualCam Pro does not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertising SDKs. Your app experience is 100% focused on capturing high-quality video without being tracked.
