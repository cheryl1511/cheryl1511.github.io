Support & Help Center

Getting Started

Open TokSort, enter a TikTok username or paste a profile link, then choose how you'd like to sort the videos: by likes, views, comments, or upload date. Results are displayed instantly within the App.

Exporting Data

After sorting, tap the Export button to save your results as a CSV or JSON file. You can then share the file via Files, Mail, AirDrop, or any other app installed on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a profile not loading?
Make sure the username or link is correct and that the profile is public. Private accounts cannot be sorted.

Does TokSort store my data?
No. TokSort does not collect or store any personal information. All sorting and exporting happens locally on your device.

Can I sort videos from any account?
TokSort works with publicly available TikTok content only.
