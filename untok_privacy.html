Privacy Policy

Last updated May 23, 2026

UnTok does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature.

What We Collect

UnTok collects only the minimum data required to function:

  • In-app purchase status (whether you have an active subscription)
  • Anonymous debug information (app version, number of launches, crash logs)

No personally identifiable information is ever collected by us or any third party we use.

Your TikTok Account

When you log in with your TikTok account, UnTok stores your session credentials locally on your device only. This information is stored securely using your device's built-in secure storage and cannot be accessed or read by us.

UnTok only uses your TikTok session to fetch and remove reposted videos — nothing else.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your TikTok username, email, or password
  • Your TikTok video content or repost history
  • Your location or device identifiers
  • Any personal information that could identify you

Third-Party Services

UnTok may use the following third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services receive only anonymous, non-identifiable data:

  • Apple App Store (for payment processing)
  • Anonymous crash reporting (app stability only)

Children's Privacy

UnTok is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of UnTok after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
