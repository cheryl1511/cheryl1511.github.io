Privacy Policy

How LikeDrop handles your data

Last updated: June 10, 2025
🛡️
We collect zero data

LikeDrop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything happens entirely on your device.

Overview

LikeDrop ("we", "our", or "the app") is a utility app that helps you manage and bulk remove liked videos and favorited content from your social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use LikeDrop.

The short version: we don't collect anything. Your account credentials stay on your device. No data is sent to our servers — because we don't have any.

Information we do NOT collect

Data type Collected?
Name, email, phone numberNever

List of liked or favorited videosNever

Location dataNever
Usage analytics or crash reportsNever
Payment informationNever

How the app works

LikeDrop operates entirely within your device using a local browser session (WebView). When you log in to your social media account inside the app, your session is handled directly between your device and that platform — just as it would be in a regular browser.

  • Your login credentials are never intercepted or stored by LikeDrop
  • The list of your liked or favorited videos is only loaded locally to perform removal actions
  • No content or metadata from your social accounts is sent to any external server
  • All actions (unlike, unfavorite) are performed directly from your device

In-App Purchases

LikeDrop offers optional subscriptions (Weekly, Monthly) and a one-time Lifetime purchase. All purchases are processed entirely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We never see or handle your payment details. Purchase history is managed by Apple or Google according to their own privacy policies.

Third-party services

LikeDrop uses RevenueCat solely to manage in-app purchase state (e.g., whether your subscription is active). RevenueCat may collect a non-identifying device token for this purpose. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

Beyond RevenueCat for purchase management, no other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are used.

Children's privacy

LikeDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no information from any user, no special handling is required.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a material way, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
