Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Overview

FeedSorter ("the App") is a utility application that helps users sort and export publicly available TikTok content data, such as likes, views, comments, and upload dates. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

No Personal Data Collection

FeedSorter does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information about you. We do not require account creation, and the App does not track your identity, location, device identifiers, or browsing behavior.

How the App Works

FeedSorter retrieves publicly available information from TikTok profiles that you choose to view within the App (such as video likes, views, comments, and upload time) in order to sort and display it to you. This data is processed locally on your device for the purpose of sorting and generating export files (CSV or JSON).

Exported Files

When you export data to CSV or JSON, the resulting file is saved locally on your device or shared through the share options you choose (such as Files, Mail, or AirDrop). FeedSorter does not upload, store, or have access to these files on any server.

Third-Party Services

FeedSorter does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Any content displayed within the App is retrieved directly from publicly accessible TikTok pages.

Children's Privacy

FeedSorter does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, because the App does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
